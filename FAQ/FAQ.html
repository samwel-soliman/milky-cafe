frequently asked questions

1. What are your operating hours?
- Our cafe is open from 7:00 am to 11:pm on 7 Days per week. Check our website for any special holiday hours.

2. Do I need to make a reservation?
- While reservations are not required, they are recommended for large groups or special events. You can make a reservation by calling us at 555-1436 or using our online reservation system.

3. Can I bring my own food or drinks?
- Outside food and beverages are not allowed. We offer a diverse menu with a variety of options to suit different preferences.

4. Is there free Wi-Fi available?
- Yes, we provide free Wi-Fi for our customers. Please ask our staff for the password.

5. Do you offer vegan or gluten-free options?
- Yes, we have a selection of vegan and gluten-free items on our menu. Please ask your server for recommendations.

6. What payment methods do you accept?
- We accept cash, credit/debit cards, and mobile payments such as Apple Pay and Google Pay.

7. Do you host private events or parties?
- Yes, we offer catering services and can host private events or parties. Contact our events coordinator at Milky.way@gmail.com for more information.

8. Is there parking available?
- We have designated parking spaces for customers. Check our website for parking details and any validation options.

9. Can I bring my pet?
- We love pets, but unfortunately, we do not allow animals inside the cafe. However, we have outdoor seating where you can enjoy your meal with your furry friend.

10. How can I provide feedback or contact customer service?
- We welcome your feedback! You can reach our customer service team by emailing Milky.way@gmail.com or calling 555-1436. We appreciate your input and strive to continuously improve.

11. Are gift cards available for purchase?
- Yes, we offer gift cards in various denominations. They make for a perfect gift for friends and family. Visit our cafe or check our website for details.

12. Do you have live music or events?
- Yes, we host live music events and other special occasions. Check our events calendar on the website for upcoming performances and activities.
